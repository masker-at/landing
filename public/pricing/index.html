Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

No, you can start your free trial without entering your credit card information. We will only ask you for payment details when you upgrade to a paid plan.

What happens if I don't upgrade before my trial ends?

Your aliases will stop forwarding emails. You will be able to choose a plan and enter your payment details to make your aliases work again.

Can I switch between the Monthly and Annual plans?

Yes, you can do it at any time. The payment will be prorated, so you won't be charged extra when switching.
